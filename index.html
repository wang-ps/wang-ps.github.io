Biography

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D candidate of Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University. I'm in a joint Ph.D program between Tsinghua University and Microsoft Research Asia. My supervisors are Dr. Yang Liu and Dr. Baining Guo. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in 2013.

My research interest includes geometry processing and computer graphics.
