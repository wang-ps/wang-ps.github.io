Biography

I am currently an associate researcher in Microsoft Research Asia. I got my Ph.D degree from the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University in 2018, under the supervision of Dr. Yang Liu and Dr. Baining Guo. I also received my Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in 2013.

My research interest includes geometry processing and computer graphics.
